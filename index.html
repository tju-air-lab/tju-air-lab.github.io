TJU-AIR-LAB Research Vision

The Autonomous Intelligent Robotics Laboratory at Tianjin University (TJU-AIR-LAB) focuses on multi-robot cooperative SLAM, intelligent decision, motion planning, disturbance rejection control, and the applications of learning algorithms in robots with complex environments. The team is led by a group of highly motivated and talented graduate and undergraduate students.
