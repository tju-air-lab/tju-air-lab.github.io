Research Vision

The Autonomous Intelligent Robotics Laboratory at Tianjin University (TJU-AIR-LAB) focuses on multi-robot cooperative SLAM, intelligent decision, motion planning, disturbance rejection control, and the applications of learning algorithms in robots with complex environments. The team is led by a group of highly motivated and talented graduate and undergraduate students. Our AIR group aims at pushing the boundary of robot autonomy and safety.

The TJU-AIR-LAB continuously looks for highly self-motivated students and visiting scholas from all engineering disciplines to join our group. Priority consideration for those with robot and machine learning background. Please email us with your interest.
